"Don't stick to one technology. Be technology agnostic."

In the first episode of Decompiled we are joined by Mr. Girish Sarwal, who is a game developer, embedded systems engineer and rc aeromodeller with over 13 years of industry experience. Together we discuss about how technology has changed over the years to become a more welcoming field and some crucial tips for people who are just getting into the ever growing world of tech.
